# QuickRide 30-Day Marketing Plan

## Week 1: Foundation (Days 1-7)
- Deploy app live
- Set up social media accounts
- Create content calendar
- Reach out to 10 local service providers

## Week 2: Local Outreach (Days 8-14)
- Post in Carbon County Facebook groups
- Contact local handyman/cleaning services
- Partner with USU Eastern for student services
- Set up Google Business listing

## Week 3: Growth (Days 15-21)
- Launch referral program ($5 credit)
- Partner with local restaurants/bars
- Reddit posts in r/Utah, r/gigwork
- Local newspaper/media outreach

## Week 4: Optimize (Days 22-30)
- Review metrics, optimize conversion
- A/B test landing page
- Scale successful channels
- Plan Month 2 expansion

## Key Metrics to Track
- Signups per week
- Bookings per week
- Revenue per week
- Service provider count
- Customer acquisition cost

## Budget (First 30 Days)
- Domain: $12/year
- Supabase: Free tier
- Render: Free tier
- Stripe: Free (pay per transaction)
- Total upfront: ~$0-12
